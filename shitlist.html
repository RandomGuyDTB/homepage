I would like to mention that the omission of a brand or figure on this list does not make them good. "Good brands", in my experience, are just brands about which less is known. I am also a human and therefore biased. You're welcome to suggest additions to this list via a pull request on this website's git repository, information about this can be found on the site index. Or just email me.

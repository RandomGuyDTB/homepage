THE GAME

""" A game, The sole object of which is to not remember that you are playing it. As soon as you remember that it exists, you have lost and must start again. """

~ James Dunckley

Different rules for the game exist; my friends and I say "I lost" whenever we lose and some people simply raise their hand. One can win the game if they go their whole life without being told of the game. Sorry! You lost.
